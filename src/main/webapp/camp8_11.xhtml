Welcome To Little League Champion Chefs Camp!

Cooking Adventures for 8-11 Year-Olds - Camp Dates: Aug 21th to 26th

Get ready for an exciting culinary journey where young chefs aged 8 to 11 will discover the joys of cooking. Our Little League Champion Chefs Camp is the perfect opportunity for your kids to explore the world of delicious recipes and nutritious snacks.

Under the guidance of experienced chefs and instructors, your children will learn to create a variety of dishes, from fun mini Courgette Pizzas to delightful healthy sweet treats. With a focus on hands-on experience and teamwork, your little ones will enhance their cooking skills and gain confidence in the kitchen.

Join us for a memorable week filled with laughter, learning, and lots of scrumptious meals!
