Welcome To Camp Scrumptious!

Where Kids Learn to Cook Like Champions!

Our Mission is to encourage kids to adopt a healthy diet by teaching them to cook nutritional and affordable recipes. We’ve partnered with Athlone Rugby and GAA Club to offer a series of Cooking Summer Camps where some well known Sports Stars will be dropping in to share their favourite recipes.

Our camps are specifically designed to inspire, engage and educate young chefs into the world of healthy cooking and nutrition. All while having a great time in the kitchen, in a safe and warm environment.

Thanks to government funding, we are able to offer each camp
for the very affordable price of just €45 per child!

This summer we’re offering classes for the following age groups:

Little League Champion Chefs - Aug 21-26

  • For 8-11 year-olds, ratio of 3 students to 1 adult and a maximum class size of 9 students

  • Kids learn a variety of recipes including some healthy snacks like Rainbow Fruit Skewers

Little League Camp!
First Division Champion Chefs - Aug 28-Sep 2

  • For 12-14 year-olds, ratio of 6 students to 1 adult and a maximum class size of 12 students

  • Kids will learn several healthy and nutritious recipes including Chickpea Veggie Burgers

First Division Camp!
Premier League Champion Chefs - Sep 4-9

  • For 15-16 year-olds, ratio of 8 students to 1 adult and a maximum class size of 24 students

  • These more mature chefs will learn how to make dishes such as vegetarian Shepherds’ Pie

Premier League Camp!

This is where young foodies are discovered! We show children the fun and the wonder of cooking. They learn traditional cooking skills and have fun with good, honest, delicious food. Places are limited so don’t miss out! Book your kid’s camp now!
