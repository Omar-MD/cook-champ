Cooking Adventures for 15-16 Year-Olds - Camp Dates: Sep 4th to 9th

Get ready for an unforgettable culinary experience at our Premier League Champion Chefs Camp! This camp is tailored to teenagers aged 15 to 16 who are passionate about exploring the world of gourmet cooking and creating delicious meals.

Under the guidance of our expert chefs and instructors, participants will embark on a journey of culinary excellence. From crafting mouthwatering vegetarian Shepherds' Pie to experimenting with international flavours, students will develop advanced cooking skills and techniques.

Join us for a week of culinary exploration, creativity, and camaraderie that will leave you with a lifetime of flavourful memories!
